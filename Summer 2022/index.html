Dear Dr. Fenton


Chair, Department of Anthropology
Michigan State University
 

2/8/2022

I am writing this online letter to express my interest in the Department of Anthropology's summer positions. I am particularly well suited and profoundly interested in leading any of the following classes: ANP 330, ANP 410, and ANP 201. I have the knowledge, the academic experience, and the love for the subject those classes and their future students require. 
                                                                                                        I am currently cursing the fourth year of the Ph.D. anthropology program, and I am close to being ABD. My teaching experience has grown thanks to the opportunities the department of anthropology has given me with TA positions and the training I have obtained from different institutions and programs at MSU. For instance, I hold a fellow position in the Residential College in the Arts and Humanities, where I study the particular elements that make good teaching. I am convinced that conducting an online course will allow me to keep building a competitive CV. I am also convinced that students will benefit from my experience in the subjects of the courses I am applying to.                                                                                                                                      I am prioritizing ANP 330 over the other courses because my scholarship is closely related to discussing nation-building processes and exploring the tensions that emerge from politics associated with collective memories. The class topic is one of the topics of my bibliographic lists. My second choice is ANP 410, a class I have never taught, but I am prepared to teach it. I took ANP 410 in the year 2020, I know the materials the class offers, and I also know where to suggest further reading to provide students with a more comprehensive understanding of Latin American cultural and socio-political processes. Finally, I am also interested in ANP 201, a course I led in the past, and a class that connects me with the most fundamental elements of anthropology.                                                                                                                                                                                          In the following web page elements, I am developing my proposal for each class and my reasons for postulating as a lead TA during the summer.                                                                                                                                                                   Thank you for your attention,                                                                                                                                                       Sincerely,                                                                                                                                                                                         Juan Carlos Rico Noguera
